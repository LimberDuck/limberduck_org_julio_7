Converter CSV with Drag and Drop and Separator Detection
========================================================

.. post:: 2025-11-27 07:28:20
   :tags: converter-csv
   :category: tools, news
   :author: Damian

The latest releases of **Converter CSV by LimberDuck** now include  
ability to open |csv| files and directories via **Drag and Drop**  
and **auto-detect separator** feature.

Thanks to these new features, you can now open your |csv| files even faster and
convert them without worrying about the separator used in the files.
